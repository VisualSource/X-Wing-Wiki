<Header title="Removed from game"/>

After a ship is [destroyed](/article/Destoryed) or [flees](/article/Flee),it is **REMOVED FROM THE GAME**. If a ship is
removed from the game, it returns all of its [tokens](/article/Tokens) to the [supply](/article/Supply), its ship card
is flipped facedown, and the ship is placed on top of its ship card.

* At the end of a [round](/article/Round), if all of a player's ships have been removed from the
game, the game ends and the other player [wins](/article/Winning_The_Game).
* Ships that are placed in [reserve](/article/Reserve) are not removed from the game.
