A bomb is a type of [device](/article/Device) that is placed in the [play area](/article/Play_Area) through a card effect
from a <Device/> upgrade card. The upgrade card that corresponds to the bomb has
the “Bomb” trait at the top of its card text. Bombs can be dropped or launched
during the [System Phase](/article/System_Phase) and detonate at the end of the [Activation Phase](/article/Activation_Phase).
