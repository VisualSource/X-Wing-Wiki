<Header title="hull" />

The yellow number on a ship card is the ship's hull value. The
hull value indicates how many damage cards it must have to be
[destroyed](/article/Destoryed).

<ImgCenter alt="Hull" src="/images/Hull.webp" />
<TextCenter
  text="Hull Value on
a Ship Card"
/>

- The amount of **HULL REMAINING** for a ship is the difference
  between the hull value and the number of damage cards it has.
